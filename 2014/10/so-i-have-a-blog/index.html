So I have a blog

2014-10-25

A lot of times have I started some kind of blog. I have one already together with my girlfriend where we write about our trips. I used to have one while at university where I talked about the restaurants where we had lunch. I have an account in a lot of services. But a blog as such where I wrote whatever came to my mind and in English, it is a first time.

As this is a personal blog, I will write about whatever I want. I like the idea of Enrique Dans, that he uses his blog as a place to prepare his classes. It is not that I am a teacher, but I read a lot of stuff and sometimes I find it interesting and like to take notes about it. Having them easily accesible is a very valuable resource for myself. If others like it, even better.

You could say there are tools for that, say Evernote for example, but they are not as fun as coding the blog / website yourself. Eventually the code will be free software, once I feel it is good enough.

By the way, the title of this entry is not original at all. I took it from Tim Berners Lee’s first post in his blog He invented the hyperlink. Respect.

See you!
