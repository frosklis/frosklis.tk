Inbox zero

2015-11-21
productivity

Despite a lot of hype saying email is broken, I just do not agree. I take pride in going through every email that comes to me. I do the same with the RSS subscriptions. But there is a catch: I do not read it all. I quickly go through the email and - if it is an automated email - I just press “j” in gmail (or feedly) or the down arrow in my work mail and go to the next one.
